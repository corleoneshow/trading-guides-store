## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Generate Your Script (2 min)

1. Go to your script generator: `https://trading-guides-store.onrender.com/scripts`
2. Enter your topic
3. Choose a script type (Quick Tip, Mistake, Tutorial, etc.)
4. Click Generate
5. Copy the full script

---

### Step 2: Create AI Voiceover (3 min)

**Setting Up ElevenLabs:**

1. Go to [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io)
2. Sign up (free tier gives 10,000 characters/month)
3. Click "Speech Synthesis"
4. Choose a voice:
   - **For authority:** "Adam" or "Antoni"
   - **For friendly:** "Josh" or "Sam"
   - **For female:** "Rachel" or "Bella"

**Generating the Voice:**

1. Paste your script (just the spoken parts, not hashtags)
2. Adjust settings:
   - **Stability:** 50-70% (higher = more consistent)
   - **Clarity:** 75-85% (higher = clearer)
   - **Speed:** Normal or slightly faster for TikTok
3. Click "Generate"
4. Download as MP3

**Pro Tips:**
- Add "..." for pauses
- Use ALL CAPS for emphasis
- Break long sentences into shorter ones
- Keep total audio under 60 seconds

---

### Step 3: Record Visuals (5 min)

**Option A: Screen Recording (Best for tutorials)**

1. Open TradingView (free account)
2. Pull up a relevant chart
3. Use screen recorder (QuickTime on Mac, Xbox Game Bar on Windows)
4. Record yourself navigating the chart while audio plays
5. Draw on charts, zoom in/out to keep it dynamic

**Option B: Stock Footage + Text (Best for tips)**

1. Use free stock footage:
   - Pexels.com
   - Pixabay.com
   - Search: "stock market", "trading", "money"
2. Create text overlays in CapCut
3. Match text to voiceover timing

**Option C: Canva Slides (Best for lists)**

1. Create slides in Canva (use TikTok template: 1080x1920)
2. One point per slide
3. Export as images or video
4. Add voiceover in CapCut

---

### Step 4: Edit in CapCut (10 min)

**Basic Edit Workflow:**

1. Import your visuals
2. Import your AI voiceover
3. Align audio with visuals
4. Add auto-captions:
   - Click "Text" â†’ "Auto Captions"
   - Choose style (recommend bold, centered)
   - Edit any mistakes
5. Add transitions between clips
6. Add background music (CapCut has free tracks)
   - Keep music at 10-20% volume
7. Add hook text overlay for first 3 seconds
8. Export in 1080p

**CapCut Settings for TikTok:**
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Format: MP4

---

### Step 5: Post to TikTok (2 min)

**Optimal Posting:**

1. Open TikTok app
2. Upload your video
3. Add your caption (keep it short + add CTA)
4. Paste hashtags from script generator
5. Choose a cover frame (with text showing)
6. Post!

**Best Times to Post:**
- 7am - 9am (catches morning scrollers)
- 12pm - 2pm (lunch break)
- 7pm - 10pm (evening wind-down)

**Best Days:**
- Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

## Tips for Growth

### Hook Formula
Your first 3 seconds determine everything. Use these patterns:
- "You're losing money because of THIS..."
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "Here's what I wish I knew before..."
- "Stop [doing X]. Do this instead."
- "[Number] + [Surprising claim]"

### Engagement Tactics
- Ask questions in captions: "Would you try this?"
- Reply to every comment (boosts algorithm)
- Post at least 1x/day for first 30 days
- Go Live occasionally (even just Q&A)

### What Performs Best
- Controversial takes
- Quick, actionable tips
- Mistakes/fails (people love these)
- Before/after results
- Duets with other trading content

## Troubleshooting

**"My videos get no views"**
- Check your hook - is it compelling in first 1 second?
- Are you posting at optimal times?
- Use trending sounds occasionally
- Engage with other creators in your niche

**"AI voice sounds robotic"**
- Try different voices in ElevenLabs
- Adjust stability/clarity settings
- Break up long sentences
- Add natural pauses with "..."

**"Videos look amateur"**
- Use consistent fonts/colors
- Add motion (zoom, pan on charts)
- Use captions (80% watch without sound)
- Keep cuts fast (2-4 seconds per scene)
